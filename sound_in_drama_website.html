What Does Drama Sound Like?

Interactive Visualizations — Sound Event Density in GerDraCor

This page provides interactive supplementary visualizations accompanying the article "What Does Drama Sound Like? Sound Analysis of GerDraCor" (Guhr, Pagel & Reiter), presented at the DraCor Summit. The static figures in the article correspond to the interactive versions below, which allow hovering over individual data points to inspect play titles, years, and genre information.

Code, data, and the full analysis notebook are available on GitHub.
